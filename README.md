## Reported Demo Patterns

From the X cases above, early community discussion clusters around these Veo 4 / Gemini Omni patterns:

- **Multi-angle / multi-camera scenes**: dynamic camera switching while keeping subjects and locations coherent.
- **Native audio**: synchronized dialogue, ambient sounds, and contextual BGM.
- **Video editing**: editing existing clips, including reference-guided changes.
- **Multi-reference generation**: combining image, audio, and video inputs.
- **Flow integration**: Gemini Omni / Omni Flash appearing in Google Flow for some users.
- **Comparisons vs Seedance 2.0**: several posts explicitly frame Veo 4 / Omni Flash as a Seedance competitor, with mixed early reviews.
